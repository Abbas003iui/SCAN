Light Spectrum Analyzer

Instructions:
  1. Upload a blank/reference image (e.g., empty cuvette or white background) in "Upload Blank".
  2. Upload the sample image in "Upload Sample".
  3. The absorbance is calculated as A = log10(Iblank / Isample), clipped to 0, normalized ≤1.
  4. The graph plots absorbance with a black trendline and a colored fill gradient matching the visible spectrum wavelengths (380–750 nm).
  5. Peak wavelength and absorbance are displayed above the graph.

Note: For best results, use images with the same dimensions.
